Strawberry Smoothie

You'll love this strawberry smoothie recipe. It's an icy-cold treat!

Ingredients:

  • 8 strawberries, hulled
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 0.5 cup skim milk
  • 0.5 cup plain yogurt
  • 6 cubes ice, crushed

Steps:

  1. Combine strawberries, milk, yogurt, sugar, and vanilla in a blender.
  2. Add ice and blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
